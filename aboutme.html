Edoardo Ottavianelli

Security Researcher and Open Source Developer. Passionate about Computing, Nature and cooking.

Contact me

Hi. I'm Edoardo, 25 yo, living in central Italy. I love Computers and nature. I've obtained a Computer Science Bachelor Degree in Sapienza University of Rome and to complete that, as an internship, I developed the End User Development system for SeismoCloud. In my CS course I've learned the basics of Operating systems, databases, algorithms and computers ( Low-level programming, architecture and networks). During the Bachelor course I've also used the most famous frontend development languages (HTML, CSS, Javascript, JQuery...), a lot of programming languages (Java, Python, C, Bash, Assembly, Go) and development tools (Tomcat, Eclipse, GoLand, PostMan, MARS, VS Code). I usually spend a lot of time in front of a monitor. In May 2023 I've obtained a Master's Degree in Cybersecurity. I use Ubuntu as main OS and a lot of virtual machines (Kali, Windows, old linux distro releases...). In the same year ('18) I've opened my Github account and now I spend a lot of time there, because I like to contribute to other projects and share with the community my efforts in order to simplify my tasks (clearly an automation fanboy). Mainly I use Python, Go and Bash. In the first part of 2020 I started to be interested in Cybersecurity, so I started to pwn things on TryHackMe and since November '21 I search for vulnerabilities on Bugcrowd. As I said, I love nature and I think it's because I live in a little country village and I kept in touch with nature since birth. The people I admire most are Leonardo, Linus Torvalds and Gino Strada. You can find some pics of my life on Instagram. If you want, follow me on social networks. You can find them at the bottom of the page.
